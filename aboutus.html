Our Story

Providing the Perfect "Look" for Your Beauty Brand

Anas Plastic Enterprises was founded by Muhammad Ali with a vision to provide high-quality empty plastic packaging solutions for the booming beauty and skincare industry in Pakistan.

Located in the heart of Lahore's paper market, we have grown to become one of the most trusted names in cosmetic packaging wholesale. Our commitment to quality, variety, and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for both large-scale manufacturers and boutique beauty brands.

We believe that packaging is not just a container - it's the first impression of your product. That's why we stock an extensive collection of jars, bottles, and boxes designed to showcase your products with elegance and professionalism.
